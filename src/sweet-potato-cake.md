### Sweet Potato Cake
---
#### Cake
- 220g Grated Sweet Potato
- 3 large Eggs
- 150g Sugar
- 90g Dark Sugar
- 140g Avo Oil
- 185g Cake Flour
- 1 1/2 tsp Baking Powder
- 1 tsp Salt
- 1 tsp Cinnamon
---
#### Directions
- Preheat oven to 180Â°C
- Bake for 45 minutes
---
#### Smoked Paprika Caramel
- 110g Dark Sugar
- 2 tbls Water
- 42g Salted Butter
- 1 tsp Smoked Paprika
- 1/4 cup Cream
- 1 tsp Salt
---
#### Directions
- Small Pot over medium heat
- Add sugar & water
- Mix well until soft boil
- Heat on low
- Stir and add tbls of butter at a time
- Add cream and cinnamon
- Stir for 3 mins, do not let it boil
---
#### Icing
- 1 3/4 Cup Cream
- 2 tbls Sugar
1/2 tsp Cinnamon
---
#### Directions
- Add all ingredients
- Mix until stiff peaks form
